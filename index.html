JAZ Travel is an free app that offers travel guides and tips by our own users and expert travel bloggers. You will discover high-quality travel reviews and travel route suggestions through social feeds in form of picture posts, text posts, and short videos based on your searches and interests. Our users share and discuss about their past travel experiences. Besides travel reviews, we also provide detailed travel information, booking for events, accommodation, and transportation services.

Our App Features

  • ⁃ Social Feeds that shows travel guides, tips, and reviews in form of image, text, and video.
  • ⁃ Trip Planner with drag and drop feature.
  • ⁃ Direct booking functionality for activities, accommodation, and transportation services, etc.
  • ⁃ Allow users to share their travel experiences by creating posts.
